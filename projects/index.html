Crowd Feed (iOS App)

Built during his winter break in 2014, Crowd is an iOS app that shows users what's happening around them. Crowd sourcing content, anyone with a phone and camera is a reporter updating this constantly changing local mobile newspaper.It can be downloaded in the App Store.

This or That (iOS App)

Built during his thanksgiving break in 2014, This or That is an iOS app that allows users to ask and answer questions in an entertaining way. It can be downloaded in the App Store, but be warned it's known to be highly addictive.

VISA Internal Forum App (System Automation and iOS Developer Intern at VISA inc.)

In 2014, Hriday took his talents to VISA inc. where he worked as a intern on the Global Tools and Auto Enginnering Team, an elaborate title for an intern working on system automation tools. Hriday worked with Chef to automate frameworks such as Redis and Django as a proof of concept. Realizing that his team needed a sample mobile application to automate, Hriday suggested to his managers that he build one for them. In two weeks, Hriday learned objective-c and iOS development and built a sample internal forum app for VISA. He used Parse for the backend of the iOS App.

Twitter Scraper (Software Engineering Intern at Trident Marketing)

During the summer of 2013 for his internship at Trident Marketing, Hriday built a Twitter scraper using the Twitter API. This could be used to filter tweets based on special keywords during an allocated time and tweet back to a specific twitter handle.

Study Buddy

Study Buddy is a website that helps students find peers to study with at a time and place that works best for them. My teammates and I entered our application into the STC Web Application Competition and placed second out of six applications and received a $2000 prize.

CollegeWiz

CollegeWiz was a project a few friends and Hriday made for fun during his freshman year at UC Berkeley. Having recently started college they created a website solely for delivering news relevant to the interests of college students. Several articles they wrote garnered over 100,000 views.

TiE Young Entrepreneurs Global Business Plan Competition

Hriday worked with a team to create a product that would help reduce the idling problem in the trucking industry. The solution entailed placing solar panels on truck trailers to power back-up batteries for appliances. His team placed first regionally and internationally; receiving a total $30,000 in business funding
